$F = \set{0, 1}.$<br>
addition is defined as xor, i.e.:<br>
$0 + 0 = 0$,
$0 + 1 = 1$.<br>
multiplication is defined as:<br>
$0 \cdot 1 = 0$,
$1 \cdot 1 = 1$.
